OpenClover 4.2.1 Release Notes

22 Nov 2017

We're happy to share with you a bug fix release of OpenClover. The major fix is compatibility with Maven 2 restored. Please keep in mind however that the last Maven 2.2.1 release was published in 2009 and is no longer maintained by the Apache Foundation. Therefore it's likely that we will drop support for Maven 2 till end of year 2018. We encourage you to migrate to Maven 3.

Bug fixes and improvements:

  • #32: Put links to OpenClover documentation
  • #25: Test compatibility with Maven 3.5.0+
  • #24: Restore compatibility with Maven 2.1.0+
  • #26: AntIntegrator and MavenIntegrator shall integrate with OpenClover, not Atlassian Clover
More details in OpenClover issue tracker.
